
# AI Safe Business Automation Platform

AI-powered automation platform designed to help small and medium businesses safely integrate artificial intelligence into real-world operations.

## Project Goal

This project researches safe deployment of AI agents that interact with operational systems such as:

- Customer support chatbots
- WhatsApp automation systems
- Access control devices
- SaaS business dashboards
- IoT infrastructure

The focus is to ensure AI reliability, monitoring, and safety guardrails when AI interacts with real infrastructure.

## Core Components

### AI Agent Engine
Processes user requests and automates workflows.

### Safety Guardrails
Validates AI responses before executing any action.

### Business Integrations
- WhatsApp automation
- Access control devices
- Customer support chatbot

### Monitoring Dashboard
Admin panel for AI logs, safety alerts, and automation workflows.

## Research Areas

- Safe AI automation
- Guardrails for AI agents
- Monitoring hallucinations
- AI + IoT integration
- Responsible AI deployment
